Civil Aircraft Notification Procedure

Alerting military and other airspace users to planned free flying. (See About & Help for details.)
Aim to submit this to Low Flying Coord (LFC) by 8 pm Monday to Thursday and by 3 pm Friday. Anything later than this may generate a 'Late Warning' and not be published in time.
