Civil Aircraft Notification Procedure

What to do:
  •  Send in CANP

  • 0800 515544 Call it in
1. Check your club Telegram etc to see if anyone else has sent a CANP for the site and time you plan to fly. If you aren't sure, send one anyway.
2. Aim to send the CANP by 8pm the evening before. If you miss that, send it as soon as you can.
3. Select your club (use the drop-down grey arrow).
4. Select location, date & times. Try to cover the likely flying window, not just your plan. The area of operation will default to a warning zone 2 nautical miles in radius and up to 2000 feet.
5. The minimum number of pilots for a CANP to be accepted is 5. If you're going, it's likely others will be also.
6. Add a contact telephone number that will be available on site. Optionally, add your email for an alternative confirmation.
7. THE NOTAM IS ONLY ACTIVE ONCE YOU HAVE RECEIVED A REFERENCE NUMBER BY PHONE/EMAIL OR ONCE YOU SEE IT PUBLISHED AS A NOTAM ON "NOTAM INFO" OR SIMILAR APP/WEBSITE.
8. Post the reference number, site, date and times on Telegram etc to prevent others duplicating your effort.
9. Pat yourself on the back for keeping yourself and others safe! ☺
